## Setup guide
You can find the manual at the source: [reddit.com](https://www.reddit.com/r/AynThor/comments/1sz0prj/skyrim_companion_app_you_already_can_try_it/)

Link to project: [github.com](https://github.com/andreyvelsk/SkyrimWebMonitor)

## Tips
- Tested and developed on the latest Steam version
- Can be launched as a web app or standard .apk
- Using [Cluster Tune](https://github.com/AurelioB/ClusterTune) may significantly reduce CPU temperature, fan noise, and battery drain.

## Known issues

- The game may have no sound on startup (same issue as [Fallout 4](/project/fallout-4)). Use the DXSetup fix ([here](https://www.emuready.com/listings/80f24173-dec8-4e44-acff-dfabc2b5d085))
