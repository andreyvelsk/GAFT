## Description
Gunther's Library for Stardew is a comprehensive, ad-free, and offline companion app designed to help players track progress, manage items, and plan efficiency in Stardew Valley. The app includes detailed databases for items, villager preferences, community center bundles, Ginger Island secrets, and supports multiple farm tracking.
